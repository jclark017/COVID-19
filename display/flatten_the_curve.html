Are We Flattening the Curve? A visualization of COVID-19 trajectories for hard-hit Western Countries.
  • Daily New Case/Death Growth is the most sensitive indicator of the trajectory of a pandemic. The line on this chart reaching zero (and continuing below zero) is the first indication that the curve is beginning to flatten. If this line is above zero, the curve is steepening. (i.e. the spread is not just worsening, it is worsening at an increasing rate.)
    A four day Simple Moving Average is applied for smoothing
    Data Source: JHU CSSE
